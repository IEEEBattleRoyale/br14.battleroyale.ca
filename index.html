Welcome to Battle Royale 14!

From intense gaming to unbeatable bragging rights, Battle Royale has it all.
If you think you have what it takes to rise to the top or simply want to have a good time, Battle Royale is for you!

Games & Prizes

Games

Every year, Battle Royale has been one of the most epic gaming competitions in Ottawa, with legendary prizes and intense games; this year is no exception.
Battle Royale 14 will feature League of Legends, VALORANT, and Rocket League, with each game featuring its own tournament and prize pool!

CS:GO will be offered as a minor, non-prized tournament.

Prizes

Battle Royale 14 offers a prize pool of $560, with prize pools of $250 for League of Legends and VALORANT and a prize pool of $60 for Rocket League.

League of Legends and VALORANT

1st place: $125 split evenly
2nd place: $75 split evenly
3rd place: $50 split evenly

Rocket League

1st place: $60 split evenly
2nd place: $30 split evenly

New to Battle Royale this year, we'll be streaming select games from our major tournaments on our Twitch channel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Absolutely anyone over 12 years of age can participate in Battle Royale. Many students from Carleton University, University of Ottawa, and Algonquin College attend, so it's a great opportunity to socialize with them.

Of course not! Simply attend the events and tournaments that you've signed up for. It's encouraged that you stay around in the server to socialize with others!

All sales are final, meaning we do not offer refunds for tickets. However, tickets are transferable, meaning that you can give your ticket to someone else. In this case, you must notify an organizer about the transferal. However, if a tournament doesn't receive enough competitors, it will be cancelled, with no ticket refund. You can prevent this by inviting your friends to play in that tournament!

Major tournaments are the games offer prize pools. This year, League of Legends, VALORANT, and Rocket League are the major tournaments. Major tournaments are expected to have more competitors, so the competition will be higher (and also more epic). Minor tournaments on the other hand do not offer prize pools. This is due to the fact that the game may not have as high of an expected turnout (CS:GO).

We do our best to avoid scheduling conflicts. However should one arise, the major tournaments will always take priority. This means you may need to forfeit from your minor tournament, though we'll do our best to prevent this! A missed game results in automatic forfeiture and puts your team at risk at having an incomplete team.

No worries! If you're looking for a team to play with, our Discord server offers a text channel, #team-formation, for you to recruit and find other teams to join! If you're unable to find a team, the organizers will do their best to pair you up with others who are in the same boat as you.

Although it is highly encouraged to get a complete product, there is no need to submit anything for the Game Jam if you don't feel comfortable doing so. You could also submit an incomplete product and potentially get guidance on how to complete it! The whole purpose of this event is for you to learn game development.
